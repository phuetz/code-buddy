2. **Understanding module resolution in TypeScript | Better Stack Community** [2]
   In practice, module resolution configuration transforms import handling from a source of runtime errors into a build-time guarantee. You configure resolution once based on your environment, and TypeScript ensures every import can be resolved before your code runs, catching broken imports during development rather than in production.
